British Shorthair

There's a beautiful breed of cat known as the British Shorthair. As beautiful as this cat is, they are a little standoffish - that is until they get to know you.

  • Average weight: 7-17 pounds
  • Average height: 12-14 inches
  • Most common colors: grey-blue, grey and white, black and white
  • Average lifespan: 14-15 years

The British Shorthair loves their family and have a good temperament. You will want to groom them a couple of times a week to keep shedding at a minimum. This is a cat breed that is ideal around children and pets. You will find this cat to be an excellent addition to most any family.
